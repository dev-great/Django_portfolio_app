About Me

Greatness Marshal

I am a software developer with over five years of experience in developing and innovating cutting-edge software solutions that enhance the user experience, performance, and quality of our applications. I am currently the Chief Technology Officer at Thurisa Labs LLC, where I lead the development team and oversee the design, implementation, and testing of our software products.

I have expertise in Flutter, Node.Js, Express.Js, Django, Vue, and Fast API, and I am passionate about learning new technologies and applying best practices and design principles to create clean, scalable, and reliable code. I also enjoy collaborating with other developers, designers, and stakeholders to deliver value and solve complex problems. I hold a Diploma in Software Quality Assurance from IBAT College Dublin, and a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science from the Federal University of Technology Minna. My goal is to use my skills and knowledge to create impactful and innovative software solutions that make a difference in the world.
